Template

FirstName Alliterative Descriptor

Age: 27

Job: Job Title or Main Activity

Background: give as much detail as possible about the person. What do they wish they had more time for? Are they already attempting to solve the problem they face in certain way?

Goals: Try and stick to one or two goals at most.

Pain Points: pick the most painful point they face most often.

Pain Level: 7 (see scale in readme)

Needs: why do they need the feature you are building for them?

Once you have finished defining your persona,
take a screenshot of it: take-a-screenshot.org
and upload it to GitHub to share it with your team!

Examples

Nelson Notime

Age: 33

Job: Coder | Content Creator | Carpenter

Background: Nelson is software engineer and digital content creator.
He works 80+ hour weeks writing code, creating tutorials and mentoring others. Wishes he had more time for hobbies: writing, reading, cooking, gardening and creating music & videos.
Also wants more quality time with loved ones and better health/exercise habits.

Goals: Have a single prioritised list that everyone in their life/team can see exactly what they are working on now and next.

Pain Points: Too many projects and tasks lists with competing priorities. Context switching wastes a lot of time.

Pain Level: 7

Needs: a single trusted system that helps focus and track time on task.

Emmet Brickowski

Age: 23

Job: Construction Worker

Background: Emmet has a simple life as a construction worker in Bricksburg. He enjoys his work but he remains unfulfilled. He knows he is capable of great things, but the druggery of his daily routine means he has no time for his creative pursuits.

Goals: Become a master builder and save the world from lord business' evil plan!

Pain Points: cannot spontaneous create something completely new without being given the instructions.

Pain Level: 8

Needs: to dig deep within himself and find his inner child-like wonder and creativity to build amazing things.

Leia Organa

Age: 29

Job: Leader of Rebel Alliance

Background: Leia spends most of her time leading the Rebel Alliance in the fight against the First Order who are intent on ruling the galaxy. She plays an active role in co-ordinating battle plans and stole the schematics for the Death Star.

Goals: Restore peace to the galaxy by ending the rule of the Empire and kill Darth Vader (spoiler: he's her father!)

Pain Points: not having enough time to practice her favourite hobby: fencing with lightsabres.

Pain Level: 9

Needs: to bomb the death star to avoid it destroying her planet and everything she loves.
